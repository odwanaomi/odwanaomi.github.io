About Jitterbug Cafe

With an extensive menu, full of options to please any taste palette, this place just makes you feel at home. Offering everything from breakfast to dinner. The café also offers an array of freshly baked pastries and desserts as well our specialty artisanal coffees. There are options for those with dietary restrictions. Its laid back atmosphere, as well the friendly service makes it a suitable place to enjoy a meal.

How we began

The café started as a small family venture inspired by a love for good food and community. What began as a modest coffee spot with homemade pastries soon expanded into a full-fledged café, offering diverse meals. The founders’ passion quickly turned it into a local favorite.
